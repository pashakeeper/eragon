General Questions

What is e-bike rental?
E-bike rental is a service that allows you to use an electric bike for a specified period. You can use it for comfortable city rides, travel, leisure, or even commuting to work.
What are the benefits of renting an e-bike?
- Saves time and energy thanks to the electric motor.
- Environmentally friendly mode of transport.
- Affordable without the need to purchase a bike.
- Includes support and maintenance services.
How long can I rent a bike?
You can rent a bike for a minimum of one month. The rental automatically renews monthly with an active subscription.
Can I extend my rental?
Yes, the rental is automatically renewed every month with an active subscription.
What bikes are available for rent?
Currently, we offer Juliet II electric bikes with up to 3000W of power, a range of up to 100 km, and advanced features.
What accessories are included in the rental?
1. Lock with mount ($230)
2. Puncture protection ($245)
3. Tires ($240)
4. Phone holder ($35)
5. Left-side mirror ($30)
6. Rear rack ($80)
7. Alarm system ($30)
8. GPS ($55)
9. Additional accessories upon request (if available).
Can I use my own helmet or accessories?
Yes, you can use your own accessories if they meet safety standards.
Is the bike suitable for riding in rainy weather?
Yes, our bikes are designed for rainy conditions. We ensure the tires are in excellent condition and of premium quality for safety. However, we recommend avoiding heavy rain and exercising caution.
